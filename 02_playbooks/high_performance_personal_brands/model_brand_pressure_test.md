# Model Brand Pressure Test

## Purpose

The Model Brand Pressure Test is the $250 entry diagnostic for models, influencers, and high-performance personal brands.

It helps the person see whether they have a real system behind their visibility, or whether their income still depends on inconsistent posting, agency access, chance bookings, and scattered opportunities.

## Core Promise

We show you where your visibility, content, brand deals, bookings, and audience ownership are leaking — then map the system needed to turn your image and influence into structured income.

## Core Hook

```text
Your face opened the door.
Your system keeps it open.
```

## Positioning

This is not modeling advice.

This is not confidence coaching.

This is not a generic brand audit.

This is a system pressure test for people whose image, audience, reputation, and timing affect their income.

## Diagnostic Outcome

By the end, the client should understand:

1. where their income currently comes from
2. what they own versus what platforms/agencies control
3. where deals, bookings, or content opportunities leak
4. what backend system is missing
5. what to build next

## The 10 Pressure Test Questions

Each question scores 0, 2.5, 5, 7.5, or 10 points.

Total score: 100.

### 1. How do most opportunities come to you right now?

- Random DMs, referrals, or chance = 0
- Mostly through one agency/person/platform = 2.5
- Mix of referrals, DMs, and some inbound = 5
- Consistent inbound from content or network = 7.5
- Predictable pipeline from multiple channels = 10

### 2. If you stopped posting or working for 2 weeks, what would happen to your income?

- Everything would stop = 0
- Most opportunities would slow down badly = 2.5
- Some would continue, but it would hurt = 5
- Core opportunities would still move = 7.5
- My system would keep bringing in opportunities = 10

### 3. Do you have a clear offer beyond being booked for your look/audience?

- No, I mostly wait for bookings/deals = 0
- I have ideas, but nothing clear = 2.5
- I have one or two things, but they are not packaged = 5
- I have clear offers people can buy/book = 7.5
- I have a full offer ladder with clear entry points = 10

### 4. Do you have a media kit that helps you close better opportunities?

- No media kit = 0
- Basic PDF or screenshots only = 2.5
- I have one, but it is outdated = 5
- I have a strong media kit = 7.5
- I have a strong, updated, deal-ready media kit with rates/case studies = 10

### 5. Do you track brands, photographers, agencies, collaborators, and follow-ups?

- No tracking = 0
- Notes/DMs only = 2.5
- Spreadsheet but inconsistent = 5
- CRM or organized tracker = 7.5
- Full relationship pipeline with follow-up rhythm = 10

### 6. Do you capture your audience outside social platforms?

- No, only followers = 0
- I have links but no real capture = 2.5
- I collect some emails/phone numbers randomly = 5
- I have a lead capture or community channel = 7.5
- I have owned audience infrastructure and nurture flow = 10

### 7. How organized is your content and asset library?

- Scattered across phone/cloud/DMs = 0
- Some folders but messy = 2.5
- Organized by shoot/project sometimes = 5
- Organized content library = 7.5
- Fully tagged archive with usage rights and campaign-ready assets = 10

### 8. Do you understand your image/likeness rights and usage terms?

- Not really = 0
- I usually just agree verbally or by DM = 2.5
- I read some contracts but do not track rights = 5
- I usually clarify usage and licensing = 7.5
- I track releases, usage rights, licensing, and restrictions = 10

### 9. How predictable is your income from modeling/influence right now?

- Totally unpredictable = 0
- Some good months, some dry months = 2.5
- I can usually predict short-term income = 5
- Mostly consistent = 7.5
- Predictable, diversified, and system-backed = 10

### 10. Do you have a transition plan for when the current visibility window changes?

- No plan = 0
- I think about it but have not built anything = 2.5
- I have some ideas/products/services = 5
- I am building toward a second income layer = 7.5
- I already have a long-term brand/business/asset plan = 10

## Score Bands

### 0–25 — Visibility Without System

You have attention, access, or beauty/image value, but the backend is fragile.

Your income likely depends on chance, posting, agency access, or personal effort.

Core issue:

```text
You are visible, but not structured.
```

### 26–50 — Inconsistent Brand Engine

You have opportunities, but they are not predictable.

Your content, contacts, offers, and follow-ups are not yet working together.

Core issue:

```text
You have motion, but no operating system.
```

### 51–75 — Functional But Capped

You have some structure, but the system is not strong enough to scale or protect your future.

Core issue:

```text
You are making money, but leaving leverage on the table.
```

### 76–100 — Asset-Ready Personal Brand

You have strong structure and are ready to scale into higher-value deals, products, community, licensing, or IP ownership.

Core issue:

```text
You are ready for expansion and ownership strategy.
```

## Top 3 Leak Categories

Use these to classify findings:

### Lead / Opportunity Leak
They do not have predictable inbound, outreach, or booking flow.

### Brand / Positioning Leak
People see them but do not understand what they can buy, book, or partner on.

### System / Follow-Up Leak
They lose deals because there is no CRM, follow-up, or relationship pipeline.

### Asset / Ownership Leak
Their photos, videos, likeness, audience, or rights are not organized or protected.

### Transition Leak
They have no second income layer beyond current attention or bookings.
