# Major Idea Signal Intake Doctrine

## Purpose

This doctrine captures how Major's raw ideas, voice notes, signals, links, repos, screenshots, market observations, and half-formed thoughts become structured HAMAL intelligence.

The point is not to force every idea into an immediate build.

The point is to preserve the signal, map connective tissue, and let the Mobs turn the right ideas into playbooks, markets, content, tools, companies, or asset plays when timing becomes right.

## Core Principle

Major should be able to speak an idea once and have the system ask:

- What is the signal?
- What market does this touch?
- Which existing playbooks connect to it?
- Which skills or MCPs are relevant?
- Which Mob should review it?
- What can become content now?
- What can become a product later?
- What can become a company or tokenized asset?
- What should be watched over time?

## Raw Thought Is An Asset

Raw thought is not noise.

Raw thought is pre-structured intelligence.

HAMAL must capture:

- voice notes
- chat messages
- GitHub links
- YouTube links
- podcasts
- RSS signals
- market observations
- business ideas
- geopolitical shifts
- client conversations
- personal stories
- product patterns
- community pain points

Every signal should be classified, stored, and made searchable.

## Signal Types

### Market Signal
A new market, behavioral shift, emerging demand, or underpriced opportunity.

Examples:
- work-from-home resurgence
- models needing career transition systems
- small dirty jobs with hidden cash flow
- parents needing AI systems for prodigy children

### Repo Signal
A GitHub repo, tool, MCP, or framework that may add capability.

Examples:
- OpenClaw
- Generative-Media-Skills
- Remotion MCP
- prediction market tools

### Client Signal
A real person or business reveals pain, need, budget, or opportunity.

Examples:
- a model needing management
- an ex-athlete business owner without a system
- a farmer selling through middlemen
- a foundation that could be restructured

### Content Signal
A thought that should become Substack, X, IG, YouTube, Telegram, or podcast content.

### Playbook Signal
A repeated pattern that can become a proprietary operating recipe.

### Asset Signal
An opportunity that may connect to ownership, tokenization, financing, acquisition, DeFi, RWA, or infrastructure.

### Watchlist Signal
An idea that is early but should be revisited when market conditions change.

## Mobs As Reviewers

Every strong idea should be reviewed by at least one Mob.

### Strategists
Look for business model, offer, market, and monetization path.

### Provocateurs
Look for narrative, attention angle, content, campaign, and cultural timing.

### Artisans
Look for visual output, design, media, product packaging, and brand expression.

### Vanguards
Look for repo, tool, API, MCP, model, automation, and technical architecture.

### Guardians
Look for risk, ethics, consent, compliance, privacy, and human review gates.

### Scouts / Opportunity Agents
Look for leads, directories, businesses, people, and targets.

## Idea Processing Workflow

```text
Raw Thought
→ Signal Extraction
→ Category Tags
→ Mob Review
→ Skill / Tool / MCP Match
→ Playbook Candidate
→ Content Candidate
→ Market / Asset Candidate
→ Storage + Watchlist
→ Revisit Loop
```

## Model / Influencer Directory Signal

Models and influencers are treated as high-performance personal brands.

They share key traits with athletes:

- body / image / performance is tied to income
- career windows can be short
- management can be predatory
- monetization is often shallow or unstable
- identity is often outsourced to agencies, brands, or platforms
- they need systems before the main window closes

HAMAL should explore:

- model/influencer directory
- ethical management layer
- booking support
- content system
- brand deal CRM
- personal brand media pack
- financial/legacy education
- post-modeling transition playbook
- tokenized IP / likeness ownership where appropriate

## Athlete / Model / Creator Unified Frame

Use the umbrella:

```text
High-Performance Personal Brands
```

This includes:

- athletes
- models
- creators
- influencers
- performers
- prodigies
- fitness personalities
- public-facing experts

Shared problem:

```text
They are the business, but they often do not own the system behind the business.
```

## Child Prodigy / Youth Talent Signal

Some young people have exceptional skill, creativity, audience, or intellectual capacity.

HAMAL can support with:

- education agents
- creative project management
- safe monetization pathways
- parent-controlled oversight
- content systems
- savings/investment education
- artwork or IP cataloging
- tokenization only with legal and guardian review

This area requires strict Guardian oversight.

## Boring Business Signal

Boring businesses are often high-value because they are overlooked.

Examples:

- window cleaning
- pool rental
- grape growers selling through middlemen
- aquaponics
- farming
- local service businesses
- trades
- retiring Boomer businesses

HAMAL should look for:

- fragmented markets
- manual lead flow
- no CRM
- weak direct-to-consumer channels
- seller retirement pressure
- under-monetized customer lists
- local monopolies
- acquisition or roll-up opportunities

## RWA / Tokenization Signal

Lux gives HAMAL an ownership and liquidity layer.

Signals involving:

- real-world assets
- prediction markets
- DeFi
- tokenized stocks
- stablecoins
- liquidity platforms
- Isle of Man jurisdiction
- asset-backed financing
- infrastructure acquisition

should be tagged for Strategist + Vanguard + Guardian review.

## Health / Food / Home Resilience Signal

Ideas around fasting, microgreens, farming, peptides, wellness, travel, home systems, and self-sufficiency can become:

- content
- affiliate stacks
- protocols
- community challenges
- retreat components
- local business systems
- productized education

These should be reviewed for both revenue and care.

## Obsidian / Graphify Role

Obsidian and Graphify should serve as the long-term connective tissue layer.

Purpose:

- map repeated themes
- show idea clusters
- connect old thoughts to new market conditions
- reveal dormant opportunities
- help Mobs see what other Mobs know
- reduce siloed thinking

## Revisit Loop

Some ideas are early.

Do not discard them.

Store them as watchlist signals with:

- current reason it is early
- what conditions would make it actionable
- suggested revisit date
- related repos/playbooks/markets

## North Star

Major speaks the signal.

HAMAL turns the signal into:

- clarity
- content
- playbooks
- companies
- systems
- ownership
- impact

The system exists so no valuable thought disappears.
